### 6. BILLING & CLOSEOUT (Previously confusing ‚Üí Now crystal clear)

#### OLD Structure:
- Section A: "Send invoices in minutes, not days" (Admin & Billing)
  - Promise: Create professional invoices automatically
  
- Section B: "Professional reports without spreadsheet nightmare" (Admin & Billing)
  - Promise: Generate reports for clients automatically

**Problem:** These sounded almost identical. What's the difference between an invoice and a report?

#### NEW Structure:
- **Module 6: "Get reimbursed without the mental math"**
  - Eyebrow: Client Invoicing
  - Focus: Automatic tracking of who paid, reconciliation, getting money back
  - Benefit: Get paid faster, no spreadsheets, no memory games
  - **Purpose:** Get reimbursed for money you're owed

- **Module 7: "Make clients feel amazing about working with you"**
  - Eyebrow: Project Documentation
  - Focus: Purchase price vs. market value, showing savings to clients
  - Benefit: Drive repeat and referral business
  - **Purpose:** Demonstrate value to build relationships

**Key Changes:**
- NOW the distinction is clear:
  - Invoice = "who owes whom" (transactional)
  - Client Summary = "look how much you saved" (relational)
- Different eyebrows (Invoicing vs. Documentation)
- Different benefits (get paid vs. get referrals)

**NEW:** Organized by designer's actual project workflow (Setup ‚Üí Shop ‚Üí Install ‚Üí Store ‚Üí Bill)
