## Key Insight

The app is organized around **Transactions and Items**, which mirrors how designers actually work:
- One HomeGoods trip = **1 transaction** (receipt, total, who paid)
- 15 things bought = **15 items** (each with photo, price, room, disposition)

The landing page should show this relationship clearly in the Sourcing section, because that's what makes the app intuitive.
