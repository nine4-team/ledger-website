# Glossary

## Account

The business workspace in Ledger. Projects, inventory, users, presets, and settings belong to an account.

## Budget Category

A spending or revenue category used to organize project budgets and transactions.

## Fee

Money owed to or received by the business for services, such as design fees or management fees.

## Inventory

Business-owned items that are not currently assigned to a client project.

## Invoice

A request for money from a client. An invoice is not the same as a transaction.

## Invoice Line

One charge or credit on an invoice. It can come from an item, a transaction, or a manual New Charge.

## Item

A physical product tracked in Ledger.

## Item Quick Draft

A photo-first capture that can later be converted into a full item or merged with an existing item.

## Project

A client engagement or job. Projects contain items, transactions, spaces, notes, finances, invoices, and reports.

## Reimbursement

A handoff where the business owes the client or the client owes the business for an expense.

## Return

A transaction or movement that sends value back, usually because an item was returned to a vendor or inventory.

## Space

A room, location, storage area, or grouping for items.

## Transaction

A record that money moved.

## Vendor / Source

The business, store, person, or location associated with a transaction or item.
