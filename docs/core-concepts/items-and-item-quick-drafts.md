# Items And Item Quick Drafts

Items are the physical products tracked in Ledger. An item might be a chair, rug, lamp, table, accessory, or any other real object the team needs to track.

Item Quick Drafts are photo-first captures that are not full items yet.

## Items

Use a full item when you are ready to track the product in normal Ledger workflows.

Items can include:

- Name.
- Images.
- SKU.
- Vendor/source.
- Notes.
- Purchase price.
- Project price.
- Market value.
- Quantity.
- Status.
- Space.
- Transaction.
- Budget category.

Items can live in a project or in inventory.

## Item Quick Drafts

Use an Item Quick Draft when you need to capture something quickly, usually from photos, before you know all the details.

Quick drafts are useful when:

- You are in the field taking photos.
- You do not have the item name or price yet.
- You want someone else to review and convert the draft later.
- You need to group photos before creating full items.

Quick drafts do not count as normal items until they are converted or merged.

## Convert Or Merge

After review, a quick draft can usually be:

- Converted into a new item.
- Merged into an existing item.
- Deleted if it was captured by mistake.

## Item Status

Statuses describe the item lifecycle:

- To Purchase.
- Purchased.
- To Return.
- Returned.
- Sold.

Statuses are not permanent. They can be corrected if the item was marked incorrectly.
